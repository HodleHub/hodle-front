# Hodle

> Hodle is a Brazilian crypto infrastructure platform for businesses. We offer buying and selling of digital assets (Bitcoin, stablecoins), self-custodial wallets, corporate bank accounts (conta PJ), stablecoin-to-PIX payments, and APIs for SaaS companies and AI agents. Everything in a single platform at hodle.com.br.

Hodle enables businesses and developers to integrate crypto payments into their workflows. The platform supports multiple networks (Lightning Network, Liquid Network, Polygon, on-chain Bitcoin) and multiple assets (BTC, L-BTC, USDT, USDC, DePix). DePix is a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the Brazilian Real on the Liquid Network.

Key capabilities:

- **Buy & Sell Digital Assets**: Purchase Bitcoin and stablecoins via PIX with instant settlement. Low-KYC onboarding using PIX identity data.
- **Self-Custodial Wallets**: Private keys are 100% under user control. Multi-network support (Liquid, Polygon, Lightning, on-chain Bitcoin).
- **Corporate Accounts (Conta PJ)**: Business accounts with regulated Brazilian bank partners. Integrated PIX, TED, and boleto. Automated compliance and KYC.
- **QR Code Payments with Stablecoins**: Pay any PIX QR code using USDT, USDC, or DePix. Automatic BRL conversion. The receiver never needs to know the payment came from crypto.
- **Lightning Network**: Instant Bitcoin payments via Lightning. LNURL-pay support for Lightning Addresses.
- **APIs for Integration**: Payment APIs for SaaS platforms, fintechs, and AI agents to process cross-border payments programmatically.

## Pricing

- DePix purchase: R$1.00 token emission fee + 1.5% service fee
- Bitcoin (L-BTC) purchase: R$1.00 token emission fee + 3.5% service fee

## Settlement Speed

- Lightning Network & Liquid: near-instant (under 1 minute)
- On-chain Bitcoin: ~10 minutes (depends on block confirmation)

## Supported Assets

- BTC (Bitcoin, on-chain)
- L-BTC (Bitcoin on Liquid Network)
- USDT (Tether, on Polygon and Liquid)
- USDC (USD Coin)
- DePix (BRL stablecoin, 1:1 peg to Brazilian Real, on Liquid Network)
